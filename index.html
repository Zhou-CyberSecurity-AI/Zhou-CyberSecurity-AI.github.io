Biography

Hi! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, supervised by Prof. Gongshen Liu and Prof. Zhuosheng Zhang. I received my M.S. degree in 2022 from Jiangsu University, supervised by Prof. Mu Han and Prof. Fengwei Zhang , Southern University of Science and Technology, and my B.S. degree in 2019 from Nanjing University of Science and Technology, Taizhou Institute of Technology. My research interests include natural language processing, AI security, and cybersecurity. I have published over 15 papers in top-tier conferences and journals, including ACL, AAAI, FGCS, IJIS, P2P, and Vehicular Communication. Among my notable achievements, I was recognized as an Academic Star at Jiangsu University and received the National Scholarship and Cybersecurity Scholarships. Furthermore, I have been awarded in various competitions, such as Cryptography and Mathematical Modeling. I have actively contributed to the academic community by serving as a reviewer for prestigious conferences and journals such as TITS, TVT, NeurIPS, ICLR, ACL, AAAI, and others.

Potential Collaboration: I am always interested in working with self-motivated students. If you have a strong interest in natural language processing and model security, please feel free to contact me.
